Auricular Acupuncture

Based on Oriental Medical Theory, the 5 points serve to balance the body's energy and assist the body's healing process. The Liver, Kidney and Lung, are seen as internal, nourishing, nurturing, restorative and supportive. In Western Medicine, they related to detoxification and cleansing as they are organs of elimination. The Sympathetic and Shen Men points produce a calming, relaxing and centering effect. Each point has an impact on Mind, Body and Spirit

How it Works

Acudetox involves the gentle placement of 5 small, sterilized, single-use stainless steel needles into specific sites on each ear (Liver, Kidney, Lung, Sympathetic Nervous System and Shen Men)

The treatment is non-verbal process lasting 30-45 minutes in a group setting. Acudetox has been shown to significantly decrease anxiety, insomnia, agitation cravings for alcohol and drugs, withdrawal symptoms and relapse episodes.Acudetox is effective regardless of the client's level of motivation.

Acudetox treatments create a balancing processing that occurs almost immediately. Most people experience a relaxing and centering feeling.
